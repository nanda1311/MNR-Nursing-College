Location

Find us on the map

We're located in MNR Nagar, Bajpe Proper, Karnataka — easily accessible from Manglore and surrounding districts.

By bus

2 km from Bajpe Proper KSRTC bus stand. Auto-rickshaws and local buses connect to MNR Medicity Campus directly.

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Is hostel accommodation mandatory?

Hostel accommodation is recommended but not mandatory. Day-scholar students are welcome. Hostel fees are charged separately from tuition fees.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

Yes! Campus visits are welcome on working days (Monday–Saturday, 10 AM to 4 PM). Please call us at +91 8455 220 100 to schedule your visit in advance.

Are scholarships available for meritorious students?

Yes. Top merit list students receive up to 50% tuition waiver for the first year. SC/ST students are eligible for state government fee reimbursement. Contact the admissions office for details.

What is the medium of instruction?

All programs are taught in English, which is the medium of instruction as mandated by INC and the affiliating university. Telugu-speaking faculty are available for supplementary support.
