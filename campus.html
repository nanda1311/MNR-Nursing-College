Home/Campus Life

A campus that Feels Like Home

More than classrooms — MNR offers a vibrant, safe, and well-equipped campus that supports every aspect of student life.

Infrastructure

World-class facilities & labs

Every facility has been purpose-built to support hands-on nursing education and student wellbeing.

Nursing skills lab

Fully equipped simulation lab with mannequins, IV trainers, and clinical procedure kits for hands-on practice.

Anatomy lab

Comprehensive anatomy dissection lab with models, charts, and specimens for detailed study.

Community health lab

Equipped for community health assessments, maternal care, and PHC simulation exercises.

Central library

5,000+ books, journals, and digital resources. Quiet reading halls and group study rooms available.

Computer lab

40-seat computer lab with high-speed internet access for research, e-learning, and digital coursework.

Canteen & dining

Hygienic, nutritious meals served three times a day in a spacious dining facility for students and staff.

Accommodation

Safe & Comfortable Hostel Life

24/7 security

CCTV surveillance, security personnel, and biometric access ensure student safety at all times.

Wi-Fi enabled

High-speed internet throughout the hostel for study, research, and staying connected with family.

24hr hot water & RO

Solar-heated hot water and purified drinking water available round the clock in every floor.

Warden support

Dedicated female warden and housekeeping staff to assist students with any concerns.

Generator backup

Uninterrupted power supply with diesel generator backup for all hostel facilities.

Beyond the Classroom

Student Activities & Clubs

We believe the best nurses are well-rounded individuals. That's why we invest in life beyond academics.

Cultural committee

Annual day, Independence Day, Nurses' Day, and inter-college cultural competitions bring the campus alive every semester.

Sports & fitness

Basketball, volleyball, athletics, and yoga programs. Annual inter-college sports meet with participation from 20+ colleges.

NSS & community service

National Service Scheme unit organizes health camps, blood donation drives, and rural community outreach programs.

Student council

Elected student representatives who liaise with management, organize events, and advocate for student welfare.

Nursing association

Active chapter of the Trained Nurses Association of India (TNAI) with guest lectures, workshops, and seminars.

Mental wellness cell

Counseling support, peer mentoring, and mindfulness programs to support student wellbeing during demanding training.
