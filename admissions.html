Begin your Nursing Career here

Applications for GNM, B.Sc, and M.Sc Nursing 2026–27 are now open. Check eligibility, review the process, and apply below.

Step one

Eligibility Criteria

GNM Nursing

10+2 with Science (PCB)
Minimum 40% in PCB aggregate
Age: 17–35 years
Medically fit per INC norms

B.Sc Nursing

10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
Minimum 45% in PCB aggregate
Age: 17–35 years
NEET preferred (state norms apply)

M.Sc Nursing

B.Sc Nursing from recognized university
Minimum 55% in B.Sc Nursing
Registered Nurse with State Council
1 year clinical experience preferred

Admission process & timeline

Follow these steps to complete your application for 2026–27.

1

Fill online application

Complete the application form below with your personal, academic, and program details. Upload required documents.

2

Document verification

Submit originals of 10+2 marksheets, transfer certificate, and ID proof at the college office or via courier.

3

Merit list publication

Selected candidates will be notified by email and phone. Merit list published on our website by 15th July 2026.

4

Pay the first-year fee online or by DD. Attend orientation on 1st August 2026 to complete enrollment.
