Programs designed for Clinical Excellence

Three INC-approved nursing programs — each built to give you the theoretical foundation and hands-on skills employers demand.

Our programs

Choose your Nursing Pathway

All programs are affiliated to Kaloji Narayana Rao University of Health Sciences and approved by the Indian Nursing Council.

GNM Nursing

General Nursing & Midwifery — a comprehensive 3.5-year program covering all core areas of clinical nursing, midwifery, and community health. The ideal starting point for a nursing career.

3 Years + 6 months internship
60 seats
Apply now

Eligibility criteria

Passed 10+2 or equivalent with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
Minimum 40% aggregate marks in PCB
Age between 17–35 years at time of admission
Medically fit as per INC norms

Subjects covered

Anatomy & Physiology
Microbiology
Psychology
Nursing Foundations
Medical-Surgical Nursing
OBG Nursing
Paediatric Nursing
Community Health Nursing
Mental Health Nursing
Nutrition & Dietetics
Pharmacology
Midwifery

B.Sc Nursing

A rigorous 4-year undergraduate degree program providing in-depth clinical and theoretical nursing education, opening doors to senior nursing, teaching, and management roles.

4 Years + 1 year internship
40 seats
Apply now

Eligibility criteria

Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology
Minimum 45% marks in PCB aggregate
Age between 17–35 years
NEET qualification preferred (as per state norms)

Subjects covered

Anatomy
Physiology
Biochemistry
Nursing Foundations
Medical-Surgical Nursing I & II
Child Health Nursing
Mental Health Nursing
Midwifery & OBG
Community Health Nursing
Nursing Research
Management & Leadership
Pharmacology

M.Sc Nursing

A 2-year postgraduate program for nursing professionals who want to specialize, teach, or lead. Specializations available in Medical-Surgical, OBG, and Community Health Nursing.

2 Years full-time
20 seats
Apply now

Eligibility criteria

B.Sc Nursing from a recognized university
Minimum 55% in B.Sc Nursing
Registered Nurse with State Nursing Council
Minimum 1 year of clinical experience preferred

Specializations available

Medical-Surgical Nursing
OBG & Midwifery
Community Health Nursing
Nursing Education
Nursing Administration
Nursing Research
